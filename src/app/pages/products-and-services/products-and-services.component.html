Products and Services

Production of tilapia (Oreochromis niloticus) and catfish (Clarias gariepinus) fingerlings.
  • AFF produces fingerlings of Nile tilapia (Oreochromis niloticus) and African catfish (Clarias gariepinus). The former is mainly supplied to pond farmers and fish cage systems.
Aquaria and Pond Systems
  • We construct glass aquaria of different sizes meant for homes, educational institutions, hotels and hospitals. We put emphasis on local fish species especially Haplochromines (fulu) and Synodontis (okoko) among others.
  • AFF is also involved in construction of earthen fish ponds and other systems. We help local fish farmers by stocking fingerlings and harvesting table-sized fish.
  • We also offer consultancy in fisheries and aquaculture including pond construction, stocking, harvesting etc.
Domestication of native threatened fish species
  • Attempts to conserve rare native fish species are carried out by domestication of the fishes in ponds and aquaria. A number of these species are already reproducing in tanks and ponds.
Sale of Male and female brooders of both tilapia and catfish.
  • AFF supplies (separate) monosex broodstock fish to hatcheries.
