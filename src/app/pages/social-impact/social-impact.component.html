Social Impact

AFF is a community-driven company and we look to impact the people around us by filling certain gaps:

  • Offer student attachment and internship to university students and graduates.
  • Build higher levels of community understanding of the projects through seminars and encouraging farm visits and offer free advice and subsidized prices for our products to local fish farmers.
  • Seek to create employment. The farm has four permanent employees with casuals being employed occasionally when there is need. Additionally, a number of youths have been trained on fish husbandry, especially pond construction, and have been contracted by a number of fish farmers in the region.
  • The number of women involved with AFF, and indeed fish farming in the area, has been worryingly low. However, a number have been involved as farmers while the farm has also on a number of occasions hired women as casuals, especially in feeding and packaging.
