Boosting Fish Production through Aquaculture Business Development Programme
Friday, 02 April 2021

Kisumu County fish farmers are set to receive a major boost in fish production after the official launch of the Aquaculture Business Development Programme (ABDP) today at Apondo village in Ahero Ward. The ABDP is an eight year programme, to be implemented in 15 counties of which Kisumu is part, funded to a tune of 14.9 Billion by IFAD.
